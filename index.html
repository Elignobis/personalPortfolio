Who Am I?

I was born and bred from the infamous Khayelitsha township, in Cape Town. I attended all my primary and secondary schooling in schools nearby where I stayed. After completing my high school, I attended and graduated from the Univesity of Cape Town (UCT) with a Bachelor of Social Science degree, specialising in Politics and Governance, and Sociology. During my university career, I've been extensively involved with several NGOs operating in and around the UCT community. I developed a passion for working with people and facilitating projects that gave back by offering adjunct holistic education opportunities to underserved communities of Cape Town.

In my spare time, I would usually explore the internet, play online games, be on sociol media, and so on. I became curious of how everything worked, especially the websites that I browsed almost everyday. In my curioristy, I discovered front-end and back-end development. I was soo fascinated that I made the conscious dicision to learn how to code. Here I am, currently in the first month of a six-month long intensive bootcamp at LifeChoices Academy, in Rondebosch.
